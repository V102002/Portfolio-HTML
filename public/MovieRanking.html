Top 3 Greatest Movies of all-time

My Neighbor Totoro

This is a movie made by Hayao Miyazaki. The visuals in the movie are amazing. The story is wholesome and calming. Recommended for people who want to just kickack and relax.

The Blue Giant

A story of a jazz enthusiast who aims to be the no:1 jazz player in the world. But no goals are achieved without problems. This movie captures everything. The laughss, the cries, the self-doubt. Highly Recommended.

Logan

An end to The Wolverine's journey. A movie for it's fans where we see how it passes his legacy to his daughter Laura.
